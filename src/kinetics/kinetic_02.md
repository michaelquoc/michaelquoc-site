I do not ski for leisure. I ski to access a state of **Forced Presence**. In a digital world of infinite "Undos," alpine skiing offers the corrective clarity of High-Consequence Physics.

## 01 / The Analog Counterbalance
Modern leadership is an exercise in abstraction. High-velocity movement acts as a **Grounding Mechanism**. It forces the mind to leave the abstract future and solve the immediate physical present.

## 02 / The Physics of Commitment
Skiing is the art of "Controlled Falling." To turn effectively, you must commit your center of mass *down* the hill. Hesitation is the only real danger.
