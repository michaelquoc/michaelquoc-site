BRAND PHYSICS v1.0

Physics of the Ferrari Brand

Ferrari is not a car manufacturer. It is a Religion of Performance. Most luxury brands sell "Comfort" or "Status." Ferrari sells "Demand."

01 / Supply Constraint

"Always build one less car than the market demands."

Enzo Ferrari’s core axiom. This creates a physics of Inverted Gravity—the customer orbits the brand, begging for an allocation.

02 / Racing DNA

The Road Car is the funding mechanism for the Scuderia.

Other brands race to sell cars. Ferrari sells cars to race. This authenticity cannot be faked by marketing; it can only be bought with blood on the track.
